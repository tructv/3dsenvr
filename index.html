Play NES Games In 3D & VR

Using an innovative 3dification algorithm plus manually created profiles, 3dSenVR is able to convert NES games into 3D on the fly and allows you to play it in VR.

Highlight Features

Immersive Vision

Realtime lighting & shadow over voxel models and customized animations will produce a completely new and refreshing visual experience compared to the original 2D one.

6 Degree of Freedom

You are basically in the game yourself

Zero Input Lag

Press a button and the game will respond immediately just like you are playing the original console

Lightgun Support

Use motion controller as the NES zapper for lightgun games
